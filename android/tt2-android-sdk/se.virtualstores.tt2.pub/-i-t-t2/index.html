ITT2

interface ITT2

Interface for TT2 SDK. 1- To get the SDK ready to work first Call initialize method. This will prepare the SDK for all other purposes. 2- To initialize your store, call initStore 3- When your store is ready, you are ready to initialize a floor.

Most of the other public attributes are accessible only when you have done the above steps.
